⚡ Technical Log: Firmware Update

The Green Code is a living, evolving organism

Detection Band Upgraded

From ultrasonic to low-frequency acoustic detection

Previous: 40-44 kHz
  • ❌ Limited soil penetration
  • ❌ High urban noise interference
  • ❌ 15-20 meter detection radius
  • ❌ 1.1% false positive rate
Updated: 140 Hz
  • ✅ 3-5× better soil penetration
  • ✅ Cuts through urban noise
  • ✅ 30-40 meter detection radius (2×)
  • ✅ <0.8% false positive rate

Why the change? Lower frequency equals better propagation through soil. It penetrates clay, doubles the detection radius of every node, and significantly reduces false positives.
